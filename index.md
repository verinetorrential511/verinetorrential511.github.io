The main screen has three sections on the left side:

*   **Report View:** This is your canvas where you build your charts.
*   **Data View:** This shows the raw numbers in your tables.

To start, click "Get Data" at the top of the ribbon. Choose your file type, such as Excel or CSV, and select the file from your computer.
